Vegetable Soup

vegetable soup is made with Pumpkin leaf; that is “Ugwu”. It's a more common way of making vegetable soup in the east side of Nigeria. Vegetable soup is paired with eba or fufu and served as food in Nigeria, this recipe shows you how to cook soup with Healthy vegetables the Nigerian way.
